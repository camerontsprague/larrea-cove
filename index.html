Our

Inspired by the harsh yet beautiful environment of the desert, Adriana Roque and Cameron Sprague created Larrea Cove through their appreciation of life’s perfect imperfections. The duo’s histories of their time in the dry wilderness shaped the brand’s soul—bringing it to life under their mutual love and respect for the land. To them, the desert is more than a barren landscape.

Larrea Cove nurtures those with a free spirit by offering a whimsical yet modern aesthetic from our collection. She who wears our product embodies sophistication and doesn’t always believe “more is more”. Our desert-inspired earth tones and natural palette cater to her refined taste. We understand she leads a lifestyle that calls for the accessibility of fair pricing and flexibility of versatile wear. Larrea Cove aims to bring affordable pieces to your closet while never sacrificing quality.
